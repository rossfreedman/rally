How to Find Player IDs

How It Works:

The system will search the league website for a player matching your input and automatically find their player ID.

Required Information:

  • League: The league where the player competes
  • Name: Player's full name as it appears on the website
  • Club: The facility where they play (e.g., "Tennaqua")
  • Series: Their division level (e.g., "Series 22", "Division 1a")

Search Process:

  1. Navigate to the correct series page for your league
  2. Find the team page for your club and series
  3. Search for the player's name on that team
  4. Extract the player ID and scrape their profile data
  5. Show confirmation page for review before saving
