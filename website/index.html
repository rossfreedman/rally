About Rally

Rally is the premier racquet sports management application designed by players, for players. We understand the unique challenges of platform tennis (paddle), tennis, and pickleball - from scheduling matches to tracking performance across different leagues and seasons.

Our comprehensive suite of tools helps you manage every aspect of your racquet sports journey, whether you're a recreational player looking to improve or a competitive athlete tracking your path to championships.

Multi-Sport Focused

Unlike generic sports apps, Rally is built specifically for racquet sports, understanding the unique scoring, court assignments, and league structures for platform tennis (paddle), tennis, and pickleball.

View Schedule

Never miss a match again. View your complete schedule with match details, opponent information, and real-time updates.

Search Players

Find the perfect playing partner or opponent. Search through your club, league, or the entire Rally network.

View Pickup Games

Discover and join pickup games in your area, or create your own for spontaneous competition.

My Stats

Track your performance, monitor your progress, and identify areas for improvement with comprehensive analytics.

My Team

Monitor your team's performance, analyze strength of schedule, and coordinate with teammates.

My Series

Stay informed about your entire division. Track standings, monitor competitor performance, and analyze league-wide trends.

My Club

Connect with your club community. Track how fellow members and teams are performing across all leagues.

Update Availability

Keep your team informed and organized. Easily update your availability for matches and practices with smart notifications.

Track Court Assignments

Respond to Team Polls

Analyze potential matchups, predict outcomes, and strategize your tournament performance.
