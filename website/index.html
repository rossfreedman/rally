Features

Smart tools for players and captains. Whether you're organizing a league or just trying to show up on time, we've got you covered.

View Schedule

Never miss a match again. View your complete schedule with match details, opponent information, and real-time updates.

Search Players

Find the perfect playing partner or opponent. Search through your club, league, or the entire Rally network.

View Analytics

Comprehensive analytics dashboard covering your personal stats, team performance, series standings, and club insights all in one place.

View Pickup Games

Discover and join pickup games in your area, or create your own for spontaneous competition.

Track Court Assignments

Master your court performance with detailed analytics. Track wins, losses, and patterns across different courts.

Update Availability

Keep your team informed and organized. Easily update your availability for matches and practices with smart notifications.

Respond to Team Polls

Make your voice heard in team decisions. Participate in polls about practice times, social events, and strategic choices.

Match Simulator

Analyze potential matchups, predict outcomes, and strategize your tournament performance.
